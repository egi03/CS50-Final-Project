What the car ?

What the car is a quiz game which will test your knowledge about body styles of cars

For sake of the quiz we will categorise every car in one of the following:

Sedan, SUV, Coupe, Convertible, Wagon, Hatchback, Pickup, Van/Minivan


How to play ?

You will be given a name and model of a car and you need to choose which body style that car has

In every round there will be five different cars for which you need to identify body style

Your results will be displayed on results tab

Can you get 5/5?
